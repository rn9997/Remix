What is Fortnite?

Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play gamemode, allows up to 100 players to interact, fighting to be the last person standing. Fortnite is a popular metaverse platform.

Is Fortnite fun?

Absolutely, Fortnite is an intense, creative game designed for 13+ as it includes fighting, shooting, and more. Many Fortnite players love the game.
